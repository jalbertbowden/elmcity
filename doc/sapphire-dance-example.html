Sapphire Dance: Acquiring and tagging Google Calendar feeds

1. The Roanoke Times lists an event at the Sapphire Dance and Ballroom Center. There's a link ("company website") to the venue.

2. The Sapphire site has a Google Calendar! Actually, as we'll see, it has two: one for classes, one for public events. Let's visit the calendars page.

3. We'll use a bookmarklet to find the iCal URLs of the Google Calendars. The bookmarklet, here labeled get ical from gcal, was acquired from the elmcity URL helpers page.

4. Clicking the bookmarklet reveals the two iCal URLs. Sometimes the addresses will be self-explanatory, but in this case we don't know which is the iCal URL for Sapphire classes and which for Sapphire events. So we need to explore further.

5. Back on the calendars page, we click the Google Calendar icon.

6. Google invites us to add either or both. We choose Add All.

7. Here we can review both calendars and the events on them. In some cases we might decide that the Sapphire Events calendar is appropriate for the hub, but not the Sapphire Classes calendar. For this example, though, we'll acquire both.

8. Starting with Sapphire Classes, we activate the dropdown (1) and then choose Calendar settings (2)

9. On the details page, the green ICAL button is what we're looking for. Clicking it reveals the iCal URL.

Notes on tagging

Here are some views enhanced or enabled by the tagging we've done here:

http://elmcity.cloudapp.net/NewRiverValleyVA/html?view=dance all dance events for NRV
http://elmcity.cloudapp.net/NewRiverValleyVA/html?view=dance,classes just dance classes for NRV
http://elmcity.cloudapp.net/NewRiverValleyVA/html?view=dance,christiansburgva all dance events for Christiansburg
http://elmcity.cloudapp.net/ChristiansburgVA/html?view=dance another way to select all dance events for Christiansburg

One view not enabled so far is one for all events (classes or performances) at Sapphire Dance. It's sometimes useful, when an event source is very prolific, to provide that view. Although Sapphire Dance isn't a hugely prolific source, let's enable that view just to illustrate the concept.

15. We'll add the tag sapphire-dance to both feeds.

16. Here's the result. In the tag dropdown we can now select dance generally or sapphire-dance speciically. And we can see that of 47 dance events, sapphire-dance accounts for 41 of them.

17. We can now also isolate just the Sapphire Events, or Sapphire Classes. For example, since we tagged the Sapphire Events feed with performing-arts, here's the union of that set and sapphire-dance.

http://elmcity.cloudapp.net/NewRiverValleyVA/html?view=performing-arts,sapphire-dance
